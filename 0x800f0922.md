Windows 11 Update 0x800f0922.md

- go to HKLM\software\microsoft\windows\currentversion\winevt\channels, and delete "Microsoft-Windows-Store/Operational". Might be a good idea to save/export the key first, so you have a backup.
- go to HKLM\software\microsoft\windows\currentversion\winevt\channels\publishers and delete "{53e3d721-2aa0-4743-b2db-299d872b8e3d}".
- Reboot and retry the update.
